# Khoj Research

## Description
Khoj is an open-source, self-hostable, personal AI application designed to be your AI second brain. It helps you get answers from the web or your documents and extends your capabilities. It scales from an on-device personal AI to a cloud-scale enterprise AI.

## Main Features
- Personal AI assistant
- Get answers from web and documents
- Chat with local or online LLMs
- Build custom agents, schedule automations, deep research
- Tailored recommendations from AI agents
- Delegate repetitive tasks
- Fast search of notes and documents, context-driven summaries
- Accessible from Emacs, Obsidian, or web browser
- Offline-first
