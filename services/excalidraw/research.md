# Excalidraw Research

## Description
Excalidraw is a virtual collaborative whiteboard tool that lets you easily sketch diagrams that have a hand-drawn feel to them. It is a free and open-source tool for creating simple hand-drawn style diagrams, illustrations, and flowcharts.

## Main Features
- Virtual whiteboard
- Collaborative
- Hand-drawn look and feel
- Sketching diagrams, illustrations, flowcharts
- Simple and minimalist drawing style
