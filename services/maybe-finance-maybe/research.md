# Maybe Finance Research

## Description
Maybe Finance is an open-source, self-hosted personal finance app and a modern finance management tool designed for tracking personal or business finances efficiently. It is presented as an alternative to Mint and YNAB. The goal is to provide a free, self-hostable app with a future hosted version.

## Main Features
- Self-hosted, personal/business finance management
- Tracking expenses and income
- Clean and user-friendly interface
- Integrations
- Detailed analytics
- AI-driven insights
